Bio

Simon Kerr holds a BSc in applied mathematics from Paris University (Pierre et Marie Curie) and has been involved in web programming and open source since 2000, when he joined Paris based software editor and open source pioneer Aurora (now a division of Business & Decision Group). He loves Python’s clean code and brevity, Haskell's laziness and smart typing and Julia's math power to the people ethos. He is based in Córdoba, Andalusia, from where he teaches applied mathematics to software engineers worldwide.
